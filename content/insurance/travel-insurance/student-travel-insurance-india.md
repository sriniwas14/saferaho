# What is Student Travel Insurance?

Student travel insurance is a specialized insurance policy designed for students studying abroad.

It provides financial protection during:
- international travel
- overseas education
- temporary stay abroad

Coverage often includes:
- medical emergencies
- hospitalization
- passport loss
- study interruption
- baggage loss
- personal liability

Some plans also include education-related benefits not found in regular travel insurance.

Healthcare abroad can become extremely expensive.

- USA

- Australia

Even basic medical treatment can cost huge amounts.

Without insurance:
- students or parents pay directly

Which is financially painful enough already without tuition fees standing nearby like final bosses.
